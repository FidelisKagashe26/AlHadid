About Al-Hadid Foundation

The Al-Hadid Foundation is a faith-driven, non-profit organization dedicated to uplifting vulnerable communities and promoting Islamic values of compassion and service. Founded by Zuberi Daffa, we serve humanity through care, empowerment, and development.

Mission

To serve humanity by providing care for orphans, empowering women through Islamic teachings, improving places of worship, and supporting premature babies in hospitals, guided by the principles of compassion and faith.

Vision

A society where vulnerable individuals are supported, women are empowered with knowledge, and Islamic values guide community development.

Core Objectives

  • Provide essential needs and education support for orphans.
  • Deliver Islamic education and empowerment programs for women.
  • Build and renovate mosques to improve worship facilities.
  • Provide medical and financial assistance to premature babies in hospitals.
