About Al-Hadid Foundation

A faith-driven, non-profit organization dedicated to uplifting vulnerable communities and promoting Islamic values of compassion and service. Founded by Zuberi Daffa, we serve humanity through care, empowerment, and sustainable development.

Our Mission

To serve humanity by providing comprehensive care for orphans, empowering women through Islamic teachings and practical skills, improving places of worship to strengthen community bonds, and supporting premature babies in hospitals with life-saving equipment. All our efforts are guided by the eternal principles of compassion, justice, and unwavering faith in Allah.

Our Vision

To build a society where vulnerable individuals are not just supported but empowered to thrive, where women are equipped with both spiritual knowledge and practical skills for independence, and where Islamic values of brotherhood, compassion, and social justice guide sustainable community development across Tanzania and beyond.

Zuberi Daffa

Our Core Values

These Islamic principles guide every decision we make and every action we take in service of humanity.

Compassion (Rahma)

Following the mercy of Allah, we approach every person with kindness and understanding.

Justice (Adl)

We ensure fair distribution of resources and equal opportunities for all beneficiaries.

Trust (Amanah)

We honor the trust placed in us by donors and communities through transparency and accountability.

Unity (Wahda)

We bring communities together, fostering brotherhood and collective responsibility.

Our Core Programs

Four comprehensive programs designed to address the most pressing needs in our communities.

Child reaching icon

Orphan Support Program

Comprehensive care including monthly food packages, clothing, school materials, healthcare, mentorship, and counseling services.

  • • Monthly food and clothing assistance
  • • Educational materials and tuition support
  • • Healthcare and medical assistance
  • • Mentorship and psychological counseling
Graduation cap icon

Women's Islamic Empowerment

Holistic empowerment through Islamic education, practical skills training, and economic opportunities for sustainable independence.

  • • Weekly Islamic study circles and Quran classes
  • • Vocational training in various skills
  • • Microfinance and business development
  • • Leadership and community engagement
Mosque icon

Mosque Development Initiative

Building and renovating mosques to create dignified worship spaces that strengthen community bonds and spiritual growth.

  • • Complete renovation of aging mosques
  • • Construction of new mosques in underserved areas
  • • Community center facilities integration
  • • Maintenance and sustainability programs
Baby icon

Premature Baby Care Support

Life-saving medical support through equipment provision and financial assistance for families with premature babies.

  • • Modern incubators and medical equipment
  • • Hospital bill assistance for needy families
  • • Follow-up care and family support
  • • Healthcare facility partnerships
