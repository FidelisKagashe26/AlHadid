Who We Are

The Al-Hadid Foundation is a faith-driven, non-profit organization founded by Zuberi Daffa in 2025. Guided by the Islamic values of compassion and service, we work shoulder-to-shoulder with communities to build hope, nurture faith, and transform lives.

Our vision is a society where vulnerable individuals are supported, women are empowered with knowledge, and Islamic values guide sustainable community development. We operate transparently and partner with individuals, businesses, and organizations to create lasting change.

What We Focus On

Supporting Orphans

Food baskets, clothing, tuition and mentorship so every child can dream with dignity and hope for a brighter future.

Empowering Women

Weekly Islamic study circles and practical life-skills that strengthen spiritual foundation and economic independence.

Developing Mosques

Renovation and new builds—clean, welcoming spaces that unite neighborhoods in worship and community building.

Caring for Preemies

Incubators and essential medical equipment for hospitals, plus direct financial support to families in critical need.

Programs & Activities

Orphan Support Program

  • Monthly food, clothing, and school materials
  • Mentorship, counseling & after-school support
  • Health care and medical assistance

Women's Islamic Empowerment

  • Weekly Islamic study circles and Quran classes
  • Vocational training for self-reliance
  • Microfinance and business development

Mosque Development Initiative

  • Complete renovation of aging mosques
  • Building new mosques in underserved areas
  • Community center facilities integration

Premature Baby Care Support

  • Modern incubators & medical equipment
  • Hospital bill assistance for families
  • Follow-up care and family support
